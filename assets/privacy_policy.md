## Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy was last updated on 12-07-2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information this app collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

### What Information Do We Collect?

This app stores the profile names you create, dates, notes, settings and any climbing or training data you choose to save. All information is stored locally on your device.


### What We Do With The Collected Data.

All collected data is stored locally on your device. The app does not upload, sell, or share your data with third parties.  

### How we keep the collected information safe.

Your data is stored locally on your device. While the app does not transmit your information, protecting your device with a passcode or other security measures helps keep your data safe.  

### Is our app intended for children?

This app is not specifically intended for children under the age of 13. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.  

### How users can manage their information

You are not required to enter your real name. Saving your climbing data is optional. If you choose to save your data, it can be used to compare your progress over time.  

### Does our app use third-party services?

This app does not use third-party services to collect or process your data. While the app uses open-source software libraries to function, these libraries do not receive your personal information.
